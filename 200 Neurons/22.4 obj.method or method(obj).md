[[12.4 Ruby|Ruby]]

## `some_method(obj)`
This method definition is used when I send arguments to the method call. 

## `obj.method`
This methods require an **explicit caller** like `a_caller.some_method(obj)`, in this case `some_method` is modifying `a_caller`.
